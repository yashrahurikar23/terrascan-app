### Use Case 1: Precision Agriculture

**Problem:** Farmers need to optimize crop yields while minimizing water and fertilizer use.

**Solution with GDAL:**
- Analyze satellite imagery to create **NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)** maps
- Identify areas of stress, disease, or nutrient deficiency
- Create variable-rate application maps for fertilizers and pesticides
- Monitor crop health throughout the growing season

**Real Example:**
- **John Deere** uses GDAL to process satellite imagery and create prescription maps for their tractors

- **Result:** 15-20% reduction in fertilizer use, 10-15% increase in yields
